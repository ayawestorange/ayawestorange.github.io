about the studio

The American Yoga Academy at The Wilshire Grand in West Orange offers private lessons, group classes, meditation, and information about the Yoga Teacher Training program at the American Yoga Academy. Our signature style is a unique blend of all the various styles of Hatha Yoga and Eastern Philosophy, much like the eclectic mix of so many cultures in America. We welcome people of all ages, sizes, shapes and religions to live their Yoga on and off the mat. Yoga is for everyone!
